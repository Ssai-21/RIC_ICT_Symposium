Where progress meets principle

The RIC ICT Seminar 2026 is an academic event hosted by students of Rangsit University International College. Under the theme "Innovation with Responsibility: Shaping the Future of Technology," this seminar bridges the gap between rapid technological breakthroughs and ethical engineering practices.

As software tools and computer hardware evolve, developers face increasingly complex decisions. This seminar brings together academic scholars, industry experts, and student researchers to analyze computing performance optimization and assess the environmental footprint of emerging tech models.

A major highlight is the special guest speaker session featuring Aye Khin Khin Hpone (Yolanda Lim), a gold medallist RIC alumna and AI scholar, sharing emerging research on responsible AI ecosystems and leading a live, interactive Q&A.

Student Research Topics

Undergraduate researchers from Rangsit University International College present empirical studies bridging compute engineering and environmental accountability.

Research Topic 1 Sai Thi Han Win

Impact of Operating System and Graphical Processing Unit on Gaming Experience Among Rangsit University International Students

This research examines the technical parameters influencing modern digital recreation. Operating systems (Windows, macOS, Linux) are paired with dedicated GPU architectures to assess their impact on gaming performance indices — framerates, thermal throttling, stability — and how these factors shape the overall user experience among RSU's international student gamers.

Operating Systems GPU Architecture Gaming Framerates Quantitative Survey
Research Topic 2 Phyo Wai Aung

The Level of Awareness of AI Data Centers' Environmental Impacts among Rangsit International College Students

While generative AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) provide instant software solutions, they rely on massive hyper-scale data centers. This research investigates RIC students' awareness of the hidden ecological costs of these AI computations — grid pressure, cooling water consumption, and carbon emissions footprint.

AI Data Centers Carbon Footprint Water Consumption Environmental Awareness
